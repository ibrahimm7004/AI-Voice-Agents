# Dine-Dialer

Develop an intelligent virtual assistant to manage incoming calls at our restaurant. The assistant should be trained to respond to customer questions, optionally handle orders, and give details about our menu. Additionally, we'd like a custom dashboard that allows us to make small updates, such as adjusting our operating hours.

## The virtual assistant should:
- Welcome callers
- Share our location and operating hours
- Send links for our menu or online ordering when requested by callers
- Address dietary needs or restrictions
- Transfer calls to a team member upon request or when uncertain how to respond
